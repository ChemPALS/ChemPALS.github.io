Unofficial Website for ChemPALS

ChemPALS (Chemistry Peer-Assisted Learning Scheme) is a new, unique, peer support programme designed to help Chemistry students achieve academic success, build lifelong friendships, and career Inspiration!

We kickstarted ChemPALS in September 2023 and are currently in a trialling phase to tailor the programme specifically for students within the School of Chemistry. In semester 1, we have organised study groups and literature review groups for students. ChemPALS is fully supported by staff at the School of Chemistry and has regular contact with industrial partners. We are currently working hard to seek opportunities to collaborate with chemical companies to roll out a range of activities and projects and you will hear from us more about it when the time comes!
